Contribute

Trelby is collaboratively developed free software, and we welcome your participation!

All discussions happen on Github issue tracker: https://github.com/trelby/trelby/issues. Before creating a new issue, please be sure to check the FAQ page first to be sure you're not about to raise a topic discussed many times before. Pick one of the issues from our issues list, or scratch your own itch!
